The Dxbians Platform

Dxbians is a digital platform that enables users to generate, claim, and own unique grid-based patterns ("Patterns"). These Patterns are registered in a centralised database and may be used as personal identity icons, printed on physical items through local makers, or traded on the Dxbians marketplace.

The Platform includes:

  • A Pattern Generator allowing users to generate or hand-craft unique 7×7 grid patterns
  • A Pattern Registry where claimed Patterns are permanently associated with their owner
  • A Maker Marketplace connecting Pattern owners with verified local craftsmen
  • A Market where claimed Patterns may be listed for resale
  • A Founder Bundle — a limited promotional offering

Dxbians reserves the right to modify, suspend, or discontinue any part of the Platform at any time with reasonable notice.

05
Pattern Rarity & Classification

Patterns are automatically classified into four rarity tiers based on mathematical analysis of their structure, including symmetry, complexity, entropy, and cell density: Simple, Rare, Epic, and Legendary.

Rarity classification is determined by the Dxbians algorithm at the time of generation or manual creation. Dxbians makes no guarantee that any manually created Pattern will achieve a specific rarity tier. The rarity system is provided for informational and collectibility purposes.

Rarity tier descriptions (e.g., "occurs in less than 1% of all patterns") are approximations based on mathematical modelling and do not constitute a guarantee of scarcity or value.
